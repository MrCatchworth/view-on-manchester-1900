A busy shopping thoroughfare, looking up towards Piccadilly. The postcard was sent by a traveller from South Shields to his family back home. ‘Bert’ writes: 1-8-1908, 5.10pm, Dear Ada, Arrived safely, have got ‘digs’ at the Deansgate Hotel, and have discovered the exam centre. Yours & co, Bert.

Let’s hope he passed his exam!
